Human Kidney Disease Multi-Omics Database

A high-availability research data retrieval platform for human kidney disease multi-omics, providing comprehensive and standardized data resources to support multi-omics studies of human kidney disorders. The platform retrieves research literature across multiple omics layers in the human kidney field—including genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics—and performs data curation, statistical processing, and routine analyses for each individual omics type. By integrating and linking curated datasets with kidney disease information, it constructs a standardized and comprehensive relational database for kidney-specific diseases.
